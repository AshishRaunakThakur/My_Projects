Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

At Ashmeera Empowers, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This policy applies to all users of the Ashmeera Empowers platform, including job seekers, employers, and visitors.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Information: Bio, location, disability type, skills, education, work experience
  • Resume/CV: Uploaded documents containing your work history and qualifications
  • Job Applications: Cover letters, application history, preferences
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, and support requests

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Preferences, session data, analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services: Match job seekers with employers, recommend jobs, and enhance user experience
  • To communicate with you: Send notifications, updates, and respond to inquiries
  • To ensure security: Prevent fraud, verify identities, and maintain platform safety
  • To analyze and improve: Understand usage patterns and improve our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations: Meet regulatory requirements

4. How We Share Your Information

  • With Employers: When you apply for a job, your profile and resume are shared with the employer
  • With Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (hosting, email, analytics)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review our security practices
  • Data Minimization: We collect only what is necessary

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality (login, session)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
